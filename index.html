About One Home Guest House

Discover the story behind Tshwane's most sought-after event venue

Our Story

Founded with a vision to create extraordinary experiences, One Home Guest House has become Tshwane's premier destination for luxury poolside events. Our journey began with a simple belief: every celebration deserves a spectacular setting.

Located in the heart of the City of Tshwane, our venue combines modern elegance with warm hospitality. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we've hosted over 500 successful events, each one tailored to perfection.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our facilities. We believe in creating memories that last a lifetime, supported by our dedicated team and state-of-the-art amenities.
